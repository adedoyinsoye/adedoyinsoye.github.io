Get Involved

“Be a part of the solution, not part of the pollution.” -Anonymous

• Post Consumer

Post Consumers under our pre-recycle Program exchange their recyclable items for “Points” which accumulates over time. At the point of collection/drop-off, the recyclable items are counted and points earned. The subscribers then use the points to redeem valuable items from us.

• Be a Volunteer

As a volunteer, you will be required to join forces with ECOMAZONS in their cleanup exercises in different communities around Lagos and drop PET bottles at designated centers to earn cumulative points. This will qualify you for free programming courses, IT gadgets, scholarships, amongst others.

• Be a Member

As a member, you will be fully committed to the cause; financially and otherwise. You will enjoy free executive trainings and world tours relating to environmental issues. Startup benefits available, if you desire to pursue a business venture in line with ECOMAZONS vision

• Donate/Partner

ECOMAZONS is a non-profit outfit, hence, we will require corporate partnerships in achieving our goals.You can be a financial member by contributing to the cause; one-off, weekly, monthly or annually. Our donors are treated to an executive and exclusive dinner bi-annually where we table our reports.
